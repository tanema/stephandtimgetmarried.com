Us.

The abridged version.

Steph and Tim met in first year of undergrad, most likely freezing their toes off, in Northern Ontario. They were good friends for most of undergrad but it wasn't until 4th year that the spark was lit. They travelled across the planet together and spent a year together in Australia, petting koala bears and riding kangaroos, until coming home later to get back to life. Steph went off to get her Masters and Tim started working as a Software Developer. They moved to Toronto shortly after and began deciding who gets more space in the closet (Steph does). Now, Steph and Tim live happily in the city known for a really bad mayor, a losing hockey team and of course, our local hero, Drake. Meanwhile, Steph is working on her doctorate and Tim is continuing his work as the CTO of a tech startup. Ups and downs, lefts and rights, good and bad, they have come out victorious as an awesome couple who are ready to share a great day with you. If you happened upon this website and are reading this now, that means that Steph and Tim love you and want you to be a part of their big day!
